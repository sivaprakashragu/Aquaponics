How to use the Chatbot

Follow these steps to connect Claude AI to your Living Lab app

1

Create a free OpenRouter account

OpenRouter is a free API gateway that gives you access to Claude 3.5 Sonnet and other models. Go to openrouter.ai and sign up — it's free and takes under a minute.

2

Get your API Key

After signing in, go to openrouter.ai/keys and click Create Key. Copy the key — it starts with sk-or-v1-.... You get free credits automatically when you sign up.

3

Paste the key into this page

Click the ⚙ API Key button in the top-right corner of this page. Paste your key and click Save. The key is stored only in your browser's localStorage — it never leaves your device.

4

Start chatting!

Once saved, the badge will turn CONNECTED and you can start asking Dr. Aqua anything — fish diseases, aquaponics advice, water chemistry, or general questions.

ℹ

To add this chatbot to other pages

Copy the <a href="/chatbot"> nav link into the sidebar of any other page. The chatbot runs entirely from this single chatbot.html file — no server or backend needed. Just open it in the browser alongside your other pages.

🔒

Is my key safe?

Yes. Your API key is saved in your browser's localStorage and is never sent anywhere except directly to the OpenRouter API when you send a message. No third-party server ever sees it.
